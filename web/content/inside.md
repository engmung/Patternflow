## Start anywhere

Patternflow is a way to play light with your fingertips.

You do not have to start with the polished version. Start with what you have, ask when you get stuck, and pass help on when you can.

## How to get involved

**Build one.** The build map starts with the 3D printed enclosure + official PCB path. Laser-cut and breadboard paths are planned.

**Fix something.** If a step is unclear, document it. If a part is hard to source, suggest a better one.

**Make a pattern.** Custom patterns are separate from official release firmware for now, but good ones can still be shared with the community.

**Pass it on.** If you received help, give help when you can.

**Join the community.** Hop into the [Discord server](https://discord.gg/Vr9QtsxeTk) to share your builds, ask questions, or just hang out.
